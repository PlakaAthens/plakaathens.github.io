About Plaka Athens Guide

Welcome to the ultimate insider's manual to Plaka, the historic heart of Athens. Our mission is simple: to help discerning travelers discover the absolute best this magical neighborhood has to offer, completely avoiding generic tourist traps.

Who We Are

We are a dedicated team of Athens-based writers, locals, and travel enthusiasts. Having walked these cobblestone streets for years, we realized that most standard guides only scratch the surface. We created this project to share the authentic, hidden, and premium side of Plaka that usually only locals know about.

Our Editorial Standards

From hidden Byzantine gems to Michelin-starred rooftops, we curate experiences that truly matter. Our recommendations are fiercely independent and unbiased. We do not accept payment from businesses to be featured on our primary recommendation lists. Every spot is selected based on its quality, history, and the genuine passion of Greek hospitality it offers.

How We Are Funded

To keep this guide free and continuously updated, we participate in selected affiliate marketing programs. When you book a hotel or a tour through our trusted links, we may earn a small commission at no extra cost to you. We only recommend platforms and services we personally use and trust.
