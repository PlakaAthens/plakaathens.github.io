The Ultimate 48-Hour Insider Itinerary

Don't just wander—explore with purpose. We’ve designed the perfect two-day plan for those who want to balance iconic history with hidden lifestyle spots.

Day 1: The Soul of Antiquity

  • 08:30 – The Acropolis Summit: Start early to beat the heat. Use our Skip-the-Line link to enter before the crowds.
  • 11:30 – Anafiotika Secret Walk: Descend into the island-style alleys. For the hidden history, we recommend the Self-Guided Audio Tour which works offline on your phone.
  • 13:30 – Meze Lunch: Head to To Kafeneio for authentic meatballs and local wine.
  • 19:00 – Sunset at Cape Sounion: End your first day with a magical drive to the Temple of Poseidon. Book the Sunset Tour here.

Day 2: Flavors & Hidden Alleys

  • 10:00 – The Foodie Expedition: Join a Plaka Street Food Safari to discover where locals buy their olives, honey, and cheese.
  • 16:00 – Cooking Like a Greek: Spend your afternoon in a Plaka home learning to make real moussaka. Check availability for Cooking Classes.
  • 21:00 – Rooftop Farewell: Finish your journey with a cocktail at A for Athens overlooking the illuminated Parthenon.
